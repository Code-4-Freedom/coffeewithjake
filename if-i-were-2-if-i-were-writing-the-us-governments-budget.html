If I Were... #2: "If I were writing the U.S. Government's budget"

Jake

I am sure that everyone at this point has heard about the disaster that is the US government's budget.

If I were to write the budget, I would have two primary funding groups: defense and transitional. 

But, what do these groups include?

Defense: DOD, National Parks, FBI, CIA, Secret Service,  CBP, Treasury Department, USPS, The Supreme Court, and the Capitol Police. These divisions are crucial to the defense and security of the nation and its freedoms. These would receive a fixed monthly budget.

Transitional: Department of Education, social programs, Environmental Protection, and utilizes. These are currently dependent on federal funding and management. The goal of this transitional budget would be to slowly reduce the budgets down to $0 while state/local and businesses move in to fill the void.

This plan would be the lead up to reducing the overall tax rate on Americans and reduce the national debt. Of course this would not be an instant fix, but it would be a good start to slimming down the national debt. In order to eliminate the debt, we first have to stop the spending on services that could be provided by local governments, businesses, or organizations.
